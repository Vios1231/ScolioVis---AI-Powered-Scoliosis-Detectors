About

ScolioCheck is an AI-driven tool designed for detecting scoliosis, developed by Bachelor of Science in Computer Science students from Bina Nusantara University for their undergraduate thesis.

This web-based application employs a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model to analyze spine images and determine whether the spine is normal or exhibits signs of scoliosis. While the tool does not measure Cobb angles directly, it leverages CNN to detect abnormalities in spinal alignment. The research, focused on AI-assisted scoliosis detection, is set to be published in late 2024 as part of their work in artificial intelligence for orthopedic diagnostics.
